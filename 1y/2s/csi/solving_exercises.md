## Part II - Calculating with Relations

### Ex 5.1.

$$
\begin{align*}
&\hspace{1cm} r \ Sqrt \ i \\
&\equiv \{ \ subs. i = r^2 \ \}\\
&\hspace{1cm} r \ Sqrt \ r^2\\
&\equiv \{ \ \text{def.} \ sq^\circ \ \}\\
&\hspace{1cm} r  \ sq^\circ \ r^2\\
\end{align*}
$$

Ou seja, $Sqrt \equiv sq^\circ$
